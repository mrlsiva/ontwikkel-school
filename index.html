ABOUT US

The aim of our school is to develop the gifts and talents at every stage of a student’s life, not just the textbooks and exams but even beyond. We believe that the best education is the one that gives every child the opportunity to explore their creativity, participate in their community, build their characters and achieve their true potential. We follow CBSE curriculum which provides opportunities for students to achieve excellence in learning.

Our aim is to create global progressive leaders. The values and traditions of St. Joseph’s Garden School is to produce young men and women who are ready for the challenges of the twenty-first century.

St. Joseph’s Garden School makes sure children are guided, independent, disciplined and safe in our institution. It is a welcoming and vibrant community, where we cherish childhood, laughter, friendship and activity abound, knowing the Why, What and How. Self-paced learning process is followed to encourage children with their interests and trigger their highest potential academically and in sports. Hope you have got few insights into the positive and happy environment of St. Joseph’s Garden School. We look forward in welcoming you to our school and experiencing our vivid facilities offered.

Love for Learning

At St. Joseph’s Garden School,studying is a pure delight rather than merely a means to an end of passing tests. Our education is based on student-led study, debate and discussion. Students are urged to learn more, to investigate concepts and to discover their own unique passions. Students are equipped to easily navigate a world that is becoming more linked.

Our instruction encourages innovation, problem-solving, active learning and teamwork at all levels, building learners' self-confidence and engagement.

Providing a joyful and encouraging atmosphere for learning and growth for our pupils is our top focus. The school's academic core is supported by the positive interactions between faculty and students as well as a strong feeling of energy and enjoyment.

VISION

Our Vision is empowering students to discover their passion for learning and reach the pinnacle in the journey of their holistic development. We aim at creating an influential ambience where children learn and shine through authentic experiences in nature to become engaged global citizens. We aspire to provide high quality education, care and instruction, guided by pedagogies including nature-based, place-based and inquiry-led. We aim to develop well-rounded and thoughtful students prepared to cope with a changing post-modern and globalized world.

We inspire students to excel in academics, to be active thinkers and to be conscientious stewards of the world around them.

Programs

  • KINDERGARTEN
  • PRIMARY
  • MIDDLE SCHOOL

The Montessori curriculum that we use is based on an internationally recognised early learning programme that is used around the world. We offer concrete, multisensory and relevant experiences. To develop language and communication, numeracy, gross and fine motor skills, emotional, social and creative skills, multisensory modes of learning are prioritised. We incorporate Cross Curricular Links, Co-operative Learning, Conference Learning, Continuous Provisions and Hands on Activities in our daily teaching and learning.

We ensure that children at this level learn effectively using innovative practices and top-notch education. Students are empowered to think critically and communicate effectively and move ahead with the thought process required to be global citizens. The primary programme designed is dynamic, challenging and personalized. Through methods of problem solving and choice determination, we introduce decision-making skills and enable them to develop their mind to reason and use logic in the process of learning. Students in primary schools also participate in an enriching programme of trips and visits for hands on learning. We inspire kids to become engaged and independent learners who take ownership of their education.

Our Middle School curriculum is designed to offer holistic education focusing on independence, collaboration and responsibility. At this level, we encourage students to be reflective learners and critical thinkers. Emphasis is also placed on creating the proper study habits, acquiring in-depth conceptual understanding and learning to critically analyse the facts in addition to being aesthetically creative and entrepreneurial. To help students develop the knowledge, attitudes and skills in a changing and more interconnected world, we promote the holistic approach of learning. We also follow experiential approach that aims at making the educational environment student-centred. The programme emphasises cooperation and is research-based. It encourages students' moral, cultural, social and physical development within the context of the CBSE curriculum, therefore preparing them to be responsible global citizens.

Admission Overview

St. Joseph’s Garden School welcomes all students of all abilities, nationalities and religions who are likely to benefit from our academic program and focus on personal and social development.
